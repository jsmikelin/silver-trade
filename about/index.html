Company Overview

Hong Kong Changjiang International Limited (香港长江国际有限公司) is a Hong Kong-based precious metals trading company specializing in the international export of high-purity silver products. Founded by industry veterans with over 15 years of experience in precious metals trading, our company leverages Hong Kong's strategic position as a global financial and logistics hub.

Our Business

We focus on the international trade of refined precious metals, serving as a bridge between premium silver refineries and end-users in North America and Asia:

  • Silver Bars (99.99%) — LBMA Good Delivery standard, fully traceable
  • International Logistics — End-to-end shipping with full customs clearance

Why HK Changjiang?

  • Hong Kong Based: Strategic location with world-class logistics and financial infrastructure
  • LBMA Compliant: All products meet LBMA Good Delivery standards
  • Third-Party Certified: Every shipment independently tested with assay certification
  • International Specialists: English and Japanese-speaking account managers
  • Competitive Pricing: LME-indexed pricing with volume discounts
  • Fast Response: Inquiry response within 2 hours, quotes within 24 hours

Our Markets

United States: Shipping to Los Angeles, New York, and Houston ports. 15-25 business days.
Japan: Shipping to Tokyo, Yokohama, and Osaka ports. Japanese-speaking support.

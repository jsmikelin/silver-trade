How It Works — Trading Process

Simple 4-step process for international silver purchases

1

Sign Contract & Pay Deposit

After agreeing on product, quantity, and price, sign the sales contract and pay 20% deposit via TT wire transfer or LC.

2

Fix Price Within 15 Days

Within 10 calendar days of signing, you can fix (lock) the final silver price at any time based on LME/SGE daily quotes. Choose your preferred pricing day.

3

Once the price is fixed, pay the remaining 80% balance and withdraw the goods before the end of the month. We dispatch immediately after payment clears.

Delivery to your designated port (LA, NY, Houston for US; Tokyo, Yokohama, Osaka for Japan). SGS/CCIC certificate included with every shipment.

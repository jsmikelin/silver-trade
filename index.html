How It Works

Trading Process

Simple 3-step process for international silver purchases

1

Agree on Quantity & Price

Determine batch quantity and estimated unit price based on current market. Pay 20% deposit of the estimated total via TT wire transfer after signing the contract.

2

Fix Price Within 10 Days

Within 10 calendar days of signing, you can fix (lock) the final silver price at any time based on LME/SGE daily quotes. Choose your preferred pricing day.

3

Final Payment & Withdraw

Once the price is fixed, pay the remaining 80% balance and withdraw the goods. Payment due T+2 (2 business days after price fixing). We dispatch immediately after payment clears.
